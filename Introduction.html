social media and 21st century education:

Social media in education refers to the digital platforms enabling communication and online collaboration spaces. There are common platforms used like facebook, twitter, YouTube and Instagram. 21st century have to do with student-centered learning and critical thinking emphasis. There are some key skills like creativity, collaboration, critical thinking and problem solving. The 21st century is one of knowledge that influences perspection in various aspects of life (Routy et al. 2018). One of the characteristics that marks the 21st century is the rapid advancement of technonogy (Siegel et al. 2019)


Social media has dissolved geographical boundaries in education by enabling cross-cultural collaboration between classroom world wide. This transformation has fundamentally altered how students learn, teachers instruct and educational institutions operate. This document explores the multifaceted relationships between social media and mordern education.
